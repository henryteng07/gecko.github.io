Powdered Wall Putty

Gecko wall putty is a white cement-based putty formulated to give smooth and superior finishes on wall surfaces. Our putty has excellent binding properties and is suitable with all types of wall paints, making it an ideal choice for protective coats.

Features:

  • Smooth finish
  • Superior binding
  • Flaking resistant
  • Moisture proof
  • Compatible with all paint types
  • Suitable for interior and exterior application

Preparation
  • Application temperature: 5°C ~ 38°C
  • Pot life: 4 hrs
  • Ratio: 25kg powder + 9 ~ 9.5L water
Suitable substrates
  • Cement
  • Mortar
  • Concrete
  1. Put 9 ~ 9.5L water in a bucket and add one bag (25kg) of powder.
  2. Mix properly until homogeneous and lump-free.
  3. Set aside mixture for 5 ~ 10 mins and then mix a second time for 1 ~ 2 mins.
  4. Apply the first coat onto the substrate. When the first layer is dry, apply the second one. Thickness for each is layer about 0.5 ~ 0.6mm.
  5. After 8 ~ 12 hrs (depending on the weather and substrate), polish the surface.

Tile Adhesive

Gecko tile adhesive is an easy to use, high-grab cement-based wall and floor adhesive suitable for all tile types. It is possible to grout & seal immediately after gluing tiles to walls/floors.

Features:

  • Fast setting
  • High grab - suitable for wall and floor tiles
  • High flex - tolerates substrate movement/vibration.
  • Reliable and long-lasting
  • Water resistant
  • Suitable for interior and exterior application
